Privacy Policy

Seller Margin KZ

Effective date: April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Seller Margin KZ handles user data.

1. What data the app stores

Seller Margin KZ is a simple profit calculator for sellers. The app stores data entered by the user directly on the device.

  • product name
  • purchase price
  • sale price
  • commission percentage
  • delivery cost
  • packaging cost
  • advertising cost
  • other expenses
  • saved calculation history
  • selected currency and app settings

2. How data is used

The data is used only to calculate profitability, display saved calculation history, and remember app settings.

3. Where data is stored

Data is stored locally on the user’s device using local app storage.

4. Data sharing

Seller Margin KZ does not sell user data and does not share calculation history with third parties.

5. Accounts and registration

Seller Margin KZ does not require account registration.

6. Payments and subscriptions

If premium features are added in future versions, payments will be processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. The app does not directly store full bank card information.

7. Analytics and diagnostics

At the current stage, Seller Margin KZ does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. If this changes in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

8. User choices

Users can delete saved history inside the app or remove app data by deleting the app from the device.

9. Children’s privacy

Seller Margin KZ is not directed specifically to children.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be published at the same URL.
